Who We Are

Axis Three Dee Studios (ATDS) specialises in the production of high quality visual content of immersive visual media, for projection mapping, holographic projections and 360 degree dome projections. It uses cutting edge digital technology and a vast experience for creating highly customised 2D and CG animation inputs, Visual Special Effects (VFX) and stereoscopic content for the immersive media ecosystem.

It undertakes prestigious projects worldwide on turnkey basis and executes them to world class standards.

Axis Three Dee Studio is associated with most of the leading names in the projection mapping industry.

Journey
